### Leadership, Activities & Interests

### Body

**Leadership**

Emphasis on surfacing assumptions, testing them, and retiring designs that fail cost/risk/performance constraints.  
- **Fencing Captain — Bishops Diocesan College (2019):** Led a 40‑member squad, coordinated bi‑weekly training, and built match strategies; finished **1st (U17) in South Africa**.  
- **Fresher Representative — Grey College, Durham (Oct 2022 & Oct 2023):** Orchestrated orientation for 100+ first‑year students; recognised by the university newspaper for contributions.  
- **Scholar‑Athlete Leadership — Team Durham (2020–2024):** **Weldon le Huray Scholarship (Fencing)** recipient; balanced performance sport with academic workload and project delivery.

**Activities & Sport**  
- **Fencing (Team Durham):** Weekly BUCS fixtures and training; focus on discipline, preparation under time pressure, and match‑by‑match adjustment.  
- **Boxing (Student Fight Night):** Structured fight camp; goal‑oriented training, risk‑managed progression.  
- **Counter‑Strike (team strategy):** Calling, adaptation under uncertainty, and communication — translates to fast decision‑making and execution under latency/throughput constraints.

**Interests**

- **AI/software experiments:** RAG/LLM systems, high‑signal retrieval for engineering documentation, profiling/tracing for reliability.

- **Creative:** Guitar.

**Achievements**
